Thanks for downloading
	"YourCraft" mod pack !

To complete installation, open the
	"update.bat" file (right-click, open with text-editor)
and change the first line to match your ".minecraft\mods" folder.
Close and save.

Then execute 'update.bat', and that's all !
YourCraft is now installed on your minecraft.
